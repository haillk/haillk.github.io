And Now: Hugo Pipes!

Haillk  published on 2018-07-09  included in Releases
Hugo `0.43` adds a powerful and very simple to use **Assets Pipeline** with **SASS and SCSS** with source map support, **PostCSS** and **minification** and **fingerprinting** and **Subresource Integrity** and ... much more. Oh, did we mention that you can now do **ad-hoc image processing** and execute text resources as Go templates? An example pipeline: 1 2 {{$styles:=resources.Get"scss/main.scss"|toCSS|postCSS|minify|fingerprint}} <link rel="stylesheet" href="{{$styles.Permalink}}" integrity="{{$styles.Data.Integrity}}" media="screen"> To me, the above is beautiful in its speed and simplicity.
